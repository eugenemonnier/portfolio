About Me

I am an Air Force veteran that’s had a strong varried career in almost all aspects of electronics. In my over 15 years of professional experience I’ve been the end-user, utilizing professional grade video production applications for network news. I’ve provided maintenance & support, diagnosing root cause issues at the component level as an electronics technician. And I’ve been a part of the software development cycle, developing test cases, executing regression tests, and analyzing results as a firmware validation engineer. Currently, I am expanding my skill set with a foundation of coding languages in HTML/CSS, full-stack JavaScript, and Python. These additional skills, coupled with my many years of experience will make me a strong asset to any company that needs an adept problem solver, prepared not only to develop their applications, but thoroughly test them as well.
